# Chisholm FC League - 10-Year Business & Valuation Plan

## Executive Summary

The **Chisholm FC League** is a competitive adult soccer league operating along the I-35 corridor in Central Texas (Waco to San Antonio). Named for the historic Chisholm Trail cattle drive route, the league provides a developmental pathway for serious players while building a sustainable, scalable business.

**Unique Value Propositions:**
1. Combined gender match days - men's and women's results determine club championships together
2. Internal club promotion/relegation keeps competition fierce
3. Built-in sponsorship and broadcasting infrastructure from day one
4. Technology platform (ballsackOS) enables future SaaS licensing

**10-Year Vision:** Build from a regional league to a multi-state operation with $5-10M ARR, potential $25-50M valuation.

## 1. SPORTS BUSINESS ANALYST PERSPECTIVE

### Market Opportunity

The adult recreational/competitive soccer market is underserved by professional-grade organization:

| Segment | Market Size | Current Players | Opportunity |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Adult rec leagues | $500M+ nationally | Fragmented local leagues | Consolidation |
| Semi-pro/amateur | $100M+ | USL2, NPSL, NISA | Quality gap |
| Youth-to-adult pipeline | Massive | No clear pathway | Development |

### Comparable League Economics

| League | Franchise Fee | Annual Revenue/Team | Valuation Multiple |
|--------|---------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| MLS | $500M | $50-100M | 9-10x revenue |
| USL Championship | $15M | $5-15M | 3-5x revenue |
| USL League One | $5M | $2-5M | 3-5x revenue |
| NISA | $500K | $500K-2M | 2-3x revenue |
| **Chisholm FC League** | N/A (owned) | Target: $500K-2M | 3-5x revenue |

### Structural Advantages

1. **Single-entity ownership** - League owns all clubs initially, avoiding USL-style fragmentation
2. **Technology moat** - ballsackOS platform creates operational leverage
3. **Combined gender model** - Unique positioning, doubled sponsorship value
4. **Regional density** - 12 clubs within 90-minute drives reduces costs

## 4. MEDIA & BROADCASTING EXPERT PERSPECTIVE

### Broadcasting Strategy

#### Phase 1: Foundation (Years 1-2)
- **Equipment:** 2 AI-powered cameras (Pixellot/Hudl style) - $5K each
- **Platform:** YouTube Live (free) or low-cost streaming service
- **Staffing:** Volunteer commentators, remote production
- **Games broadcast:** 20% of games (showcase matches)
- **Revenue model:** Free with sponsor overlays

**Year 1-2 Broadcasting Costs:**
- Equipment (amortized): $2,500/year
- Streaming platform: $1,200/year
- Graphics/overlays: $2,000 setup
- **Total:** ~$5,000/year

**Year 1-2 Broadcasting Revenue:**
- Sponsor overlays: $5,000
- **Net:** Break-even to slight positive

#### Phase 2: Growth (Years 3-5)
- **Equipment:** 4-6 cameras at primary venues, permanent installation
- **Platform:** Custom streaming portal with PPV/subscription options
- **Staffing:** Part-time broadcast coordinator, trained volunteer crew
- **Games broadcast:** 50% of games
- **Revenue model:** Free tier (ads) + Premium tier ($5/month or $3/game PPV)

**Year 3-5 Broadcasting Costs:**
- Equipment (amortized): $8,000/year
- Platform/CDN: $6,000/year
- Staff: $15,000/year
- Production: $10,000/year
- **Total:** ~$40,000/year

**Year 3-5 Broadcasting Revenue:**
- Sponsor overlays/pre-roll: $30,000
- Subscriptions (500 × $50/year): $25,000
- PPV (playoff games): $10,000
- **Total:** $65,000/year
- **Net:** +$25,000/year

#### Phase 3: Scale (Years 6-10)
- **Equipment:** Full broadcast infrastructure at all venues
- **Platform:** Branded OTT app (iOS, Android, Smart TV)
- **Staffing:** Full broadcast team (2-3 FTE)
- **Games broadcast:** 100% of games
- **Revenue model:** Tiered subscriptions + advertising + potential regional TV deal

**Year 10 Broadcasting Revenue:**
- Subscriptions (3,000 × $60/year): $180,000
- Advertising/CPM: $150,000
- Sponsor integrations: $100,000
- Regional TV licensing: $50,000+
- **Total:** $500,000+

## 6. M&A / VALUATION SPECIALIST PERSPECTIVE

### Valuation Methodology

Sports properties are valued using multiple methodologies:

| Method | Multiple | Application |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Revenue Multiple** | 3-5x | Standard for minor leagues |
| **EBITDA Multiple** | 6-10x | Profitable operations |
| **ARR Multiple (SaaS)** | 5-7x | Platform licensing component |
| **Comparable Transactions** | Varies | Recent league sales |

### Valuation Scenarios (Year 10)

#### Conservative Scenario
```
Revenue: $4.0M
EBITDA: $1.5M
Multiple: 4x Revenue OR 8x EBITDA

VALUATION: $12-16M
```

#### Base Scenario
```
Revenue: $4.75M
EBITDA: $2.0M
Platform ARR: $500K
Multiple: 4.5x Revenue + 5x Platform ARR

VALUATION: $21M + $2.5M = $23.5M
```

#### Aggressive Scenario
```
Revenue: $6.0M
EBITDA: $2.5M
Platform ARR: $800K
Multiple: 5x Revenue + 6x Platform ARR

VALUATION: $30M + $4.8M = $35M
```

### Exit Scenarios

| Scenario | Buyer Type | Rationale | Likely Valuation |
|----------|------------|-----------|------------------|
| **Strategic Sale** | USL, MLS, or sports media company | Market entry, content acquisition | 5-7x revenue |
| **Private Equity** | Sports-focused PE fund | Roll-up strategy, platform value | 4-6x revenue |
| **Platform Sale** | Sports tech company (TeamSnap, SportsEngine) | Technology acquisition | 6-8x ARR |
| **Franchise Conversion** | Sell clubs to local owners | Capital recapture, ongoing royalties | 2-3x per franchise |
| **Hold & Dividend** | Retain ownership | Cash flow, lifestyle business | N/A |
